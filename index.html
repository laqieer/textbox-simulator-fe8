Glyphs are rendered from the real FE8 16×16 2bpp bitmaps with advance widths straight from the decomp. Each font group switches glyphs, palette, and window together:

  • Talk (dialogue): shaded through the dialogue palette (gPal_HelpTextBox @ colour 0xb, LUT gFontgrp_14): 0 transparent, 1 #DEDEDE, 2 #BDBDBD, 3 #292929, in the real Img_TalkBubble frame (Pal_TalkBubble).
  • System (menus/help): the help-box/menu font shaded through TEXT_COLOR_SYSTEM_WHITE (TEXT_COLOR_0123, identity LUT gFontgrp_3) over Pal_Text: 0 transparent, 1 #A58CDE, 2 #FFFFFF, 3 #3A3129 — white-on-dark, in the real FE8 menu window (uiutils.c DrawUiFrame, gUiFrameImage + gUiFramePaletteA).
